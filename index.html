GIRAFES vs SEA

Help the Going Giraffes crew and its captain defeat the evils of the sea with math!

GIRAFES vs SEA

INSTRUCTIONS

Fill the "type result here..." with the correct result to drive the deadly animals away!
You can answer the result to any type of animal that appears on your screen!
You will start with 100 hit points (HP) and if any animal reaches your ship, you'll lose HP!
If your ship's HP reaches 0, the ship will drown and you'll lose...
Each animal has its own strength and speed values that will be determined by how hard the math problem is!
Dumb animals are fast but deal a low amount of damage and are easily scared away with simple math,
while intelligent ones are slower but deal a lot more damage and are not that easily scared!
