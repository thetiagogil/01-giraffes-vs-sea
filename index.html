GIRAFFES vs SEA

Help the crew defend the ship from sea enemies by solving math before they reach the deck.

Game by: Tiago Gil, 2023

Instructions

Type the correct result and submit it to scare away any active sea enemy. More than one enemy can be on screen, and your answer can defeat whichever enemy matches it.

The ship starts with 100 HP. If an enemy reaches the ship, it deals damage and leaves the field. Keep the ship above 0 HP until every enemy is gone.

Faster enemies have simpler problems. Slower enemies hit harder and bring bigger numbers.
